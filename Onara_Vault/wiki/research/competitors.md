# Competitor Analysis

Who Onara competes with and why each comparison is favorable.

---

## Direct Competitors

### Wix / Squarespace / Weebly

**What they are**: DIY drag-and-drop website builders. The default answer when someone says "I need a website."

**Why SMB owners don't convert**:
- Requires hours of design decisions (colors, fonts, layout, copy)
- Non-technical users get stuck at the blank canvas
- Many pay, never finish, and cancel
- Template-based: results look generic, not specific to their business
- No Google Business Profile integration — all data entered manually

**Onara's position**: Zero design decisions. Search your Google listing → site is done in 60 seconds. This is the core differentiation.

**Their counter**: Brand awareness, pricing, and ecosystem (Wix App Market). They win on awareness, not product fit.

---

### Webflow

**What they are**: Professional visual web builder, used mainly by designers and agencies.

**Why irrelevant for Onara's ICP**: Overkill for a plumber. Steep learning curve. Designed for designers, not business owners.

---

### GoDaddy Websites + Marketing

**What they are**: Domain-first hosting with website builder bundled. Known for aggressive upsells.

**Why users distrust them**: SMB owners have been burned by GoDaddy pricing tricks and poor support. "No credit card required" directly counters GoDaddy's capture-and-lock model.

**Onara's position**: Trust messaging + clean pricing. "$12/month, cancel any time, no tricks."

---

### Lokal / LocalSites / Yell / Hibu

**What they are**: Done-for-you website services for local businesses. An agency builds your site.

**Price point**: $300–$800 upfront + $50–200/month maintenance.

**Onara's position**: Same quality result in 60 seconds for $12/month. No waiting 2 weeks for a human to design your site.

**Their counter**: High-touch relationship, phone support, "someone you can call." Onara's free tier + trial + clear pricing is the counter.

---

## Indirect Competitors

### Google Business Profile (GBP) itself

**The risk**: "I don't need a website — I have a Google listing." Many contractors have this objection.

**Counter**: GBP doesn't rank in regular Google search. A website gets you into normal search results beyond GBP. And Onara's site links *back* to the GBP to strengthen both. They're complementary, not competing.

---

### Linktree / Carrd / Notion Pages

**What they are**: Simple single-page link aggregators or lightweight sites. Free or near-free.

**Who uses them**: Side hustlers, creators, freelancers who want a minimal web presence.

**Onara's position**: These don't rank in local search. A plumber's Carrd page won't appear when someone searches "plumber near me." Onara generates SEO-optimized sites with LocalBusiness schema, geo meta tags, and keyword-targeted copy.

## Competitive Moat (Current)

1. **GBP-native**: Pulls real data from Google — no competitor does this natively
2. **60-second generation**: No human-in-the-loop — instant gratification beats "book a call"
3. **Contractor-specific AI**: Copy and design tuned for plumbers, HVAC, electricians — not generic

---

## Competitive Moat (v2+)

1. **Auto-sync with GBP**: Site stays current without user action — no competitor offers this
2. **Seasonal SEO pages**: Site grows over time automatically — a DIY builder requires the user to do this
3. **Review badge auto-refresh**: Live Google star rating on the site — requires ongoing API access competitors won't bother with

## Pricing Positioning

Onara is below Wix and GoDaddy's comparable tiers while delivering a significantly better result for the non-technical SMB owner. The key message is not "cheaper" — it is "better for your specific situation."

Framing:
- Not: "We're cheaper than Wix"
- Yes: "You'll have a live website before the coffee you're drinking right now gets cold"
