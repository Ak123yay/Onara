## 30-Minute Lurk Protocol

Spend at least 30 minutes per group reading posts before posting anything. This is research, not outreach.

### What to look for

**Pain points (capture verbatim quotes)**
- Complaints about getting leads ("slow season", "can't find work", "need more calls")
- Complaints about websites ("my website is trash", "don't even have one", "Wix is killing me")
- Complaints about reviews ("trying to get more Google reviews", "bad review ruined me")
- Pricing anxiety ("customers always getting 3 quotes", "lowball competition")

**Digital presence signals**
- Does the contractor have a website in their profile? (note: yes/no)
- Do they post a phone number instead of a website link?
- Do they link to Facebook instead of an independent site?
- Do they say "DM me" or "call/text" rather than "visit my site"?

**Competitor mentions**
- Any mention of Wix, Squarespace, Weebly, GoDaddy, or web designers
- Any complaints about those platforms or their costs
- Any mention of what they currently pay for digital tools

**Conversation patterns**
- How do contractors describe their business?
- What questions do homeowners ask that contractors struggle to answer?
- What does word-of-mouth look like? ("call Mike, he did my roof", "text me your number")

### What to record

After each 30-min session, add a row to `output/facebook-lurk-notes.md`:
- Group name
- Date visited
- Top 3 observed pain points
- Rough count of active contractors in recent posts
- Any verbatim quote worth saving
- Contractor names to check for GBP + no website

---

## Outreach (After Lurk Phase — Do Not Rush)

Only post or DM after 1–2 weeks of passive observation. When ready:
- Lead with value: "I noticed a lot of you mention [pain point] — I built something that helps with this"
- Never pitch in comments; use DM only
- Offer 14-day free trial, no card required
- Frame around leads and calls, not "a website"
