# Go-to-Market Strategy

Launch execution plan for reaching the first 50 paying users with zero marketing budget.

---

## Launch Target

**Metro area**: Washington DC / Northern Virginia (committed 2026-05-15). Covers DC proper + Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, Loudoun, Prince William counties. High household income, dense contractor market, English-speaking.

**Why one city**:
- Easier to find no-website GBP listings at scale
- Can join local Facebook groups specific to that metro
- Testimonials from local users are more credible to local prospects
- Can do in-person demos if needed

---

## Primary Channel — Cold Outbound via Google Maps

This is the highest-ROI channel at launch. No ad spend. Targets people with a proven need.

**Finding leads**:
1. Open Google Maps in the target metro
2. Search: `plumber`, `electrician`, `hvac`, `landscaper`, `roofer`, `handyman`
3. Filter for businesses with no website (the "website" field is blank in the listing)
4. Export to a spreadsheet: business name, phone number, Google listing URL

**Target list**: 200+ no-website listings in the target metro before launch

**Outreach**:
- Primary: Facebook DM to the business's Facebook page (most contractors have FB even without a website)
- Secondary: Cold email if email is in the GBP listing
- Tertiary: Cold call (last resort — low response rate, high time cost)

See `wiki/content/outbound-scripts.md` for exact message templates.

---

## Second Channel — Contractor Facebook Groups

**Groups to target**: Local trade/contractor groups in the target metro. Examples:
- "DC / Northern Virginia Contractors"
- "Northern Virginia Home Services Pros"
- Local HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, or handyman association groups

**Strategy**:
1. Join as a member, read for 30 min to understand the culture
2. Engage genuinely for 1–2 weeks before posting anything promotional
3. Post value-first content: "I noticed most of you don't have a website — here's why that's costing you leads" + data
4. Offer free trials (already built-in) — no need for a special offer

**Time commitment**: 30 min/day during first month

---

## Third Channel — Accountant Referral Network

Accountants for small businesses see every client's pain points. An accountant who refers a contractor to Onara earns 20% MRR for 12 months ($2.40/month at Starter, $5.80/month at Pro, per referral).

**Target**: Local accounting firms and bookkeepers who serve contractors

**Pitch**: "Your contractor clients need websites. I'll pay you 20% recurring for every referral that converts. I'll do all the follow-up — you just forward them the link."

This channel has a long sales cycle but high referral quality (accountant-endorsed = already trusted).

See `wiki/business/distribution.md` for full channel detail.

## Launch Sequence

**Week -4 (before launch)**:
- [ ] 200+ no-website leads identified in target metro
- [ ] Facebook groups joined, lurking period started
- [ ] 5 accountants contacted about referral program
- [ ] 20-person warm list (friends/family of founder with relevant businesses or connections) assembled
- [ ] Resend email sequences ready (welcome, Day 11, Day 13, Day 14)

**Week -2**:
- [ ] Soft launch to warm list (20 people)
- [ ] Get 3–5 real testimonials from warm list
- [ ] Fix any bugs surfaced by warm list usage

**Week 0 (launch)**:
- [ ] Begin cold outbound (10–20 messages/day)
- [ ] First Facebook group value post
- [ ] Referral program email to accountants

**Week 1–4**:
- [ ] Continue outbound 10–20/day
- [ ] Respond to all DMs within 1 hour (early users are the highest value)
- [ ] Iterate on messaging based on objections heard
- [ ] Track conversion by channel in a spreadsheet

## Conversion Math

| Channel | Reach/week | Response rate | Trial rate | Conversion |
|---------|-----------|--------------|------------|-----------|
| Cold outbound (GBP) | 100 messages | 5–10% | 50% of responses | 2–3 new trials/week |
| Facebook groups | Varies | Organic | Varies | 1–2 new trials/week |
| Accountant referrals | 1–2 referrals | High trust | 70% | 1 new trial every 2 weeks |

**Target**: 10 new trials/week from Week 2 → 5–10% of 40 trials by Day 14 = 2–4 new paid users/week → $24–$116/week new MRR

**$1K MRR target**: ~50 paid users. At 3 new paid/week = reached by Week 17 (4 months).

## Messaging Principles

1. **Lead with the pain, not the product**: "Does your business show up online when someone searches for a plumber in [city]?"

2. **60 seconds is the hook**: The timeline is the story. Don't explain AI agents — say "60 seconds, no design work, no technical knowledge."

3. **Trust signals upfront**: "No credit card. 14-day full access. Cancel any time." Address the Wix/GoDaddy trauma directly.

4. **Specificity beats generic**: "A site for plumbers in Arlington" beats "a site for small businesses." Be hyper-specific to the vertical.

5. **Social proof as soon as possible**: Even 3 testimonials from local contractors change everything. Prioritize getting real users, not perfect users.
